Redefining the Window & Door Buying Experience

Est 2025

Our Story

For decades, window and door replacement has felt like a chore. You schedule an appointment, set aside an hour or more, make sure every homeowner is present, and brace yourself for the sales pitch that follows. The process feels outdated — built on tactics from the 1960s — where your time and comfort take a back seat to closing a deal.

At UpSky, we're changing that. We believe homeowners deserve transparency, convenience, and respect for their time. Instead of sending a sales rep into your home, we give you control. Simply take photos of the areas you'd like replaced, upload them through our website, and our experts will send a detailed quote straight to your inbox — no pushy follow-ups, no pressure, no strings attached.

In nearly every other industry, a quick online search can give you a clear idea of what something should cost. Unfortunately, that hasn’t been true in the window and door business — until now. We’re redefining what it means to shop for windows: transparent pricing, professional service, and a process built entirely around you.
